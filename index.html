About Us

At Mobile Campus, we believe that learning should not be confined only to the four walls of the classroom.

We want students to experience learning wherever they are, be it discovering the power of words on a billboard advertisement, appreciating the poetry of language through a song, or practicing algebra at the supermarket!

We have a team of passionate, caring and dedicated teachers who aim to make learning fun for our students. We hope to inculcate in our students the values of curiosity, creativity and a motivation to learn. At Mobile Campus, learning is organic, compelling and enjoyable!

What we offer:

  • MOE trained and qualified teachers
  • Pedagogy based on current academic research.
  • Focused one-on-one lessons
  • Customised curriculum with a tailored approach
  • Rigorous yet engaging sessions
  • Free trial lesson

With our effective online lessons, your child can learn in the comfort of their own learning space, wherever that may be! Accepting applications for Primary and Secondary School English intake today! Chat with us today to find out more!

Is the curriculum at Mobile Campus suitable for my child?

The curriculum at Mobile Campus is tailored to the learning needs of your child. While there are specific learning outcomes designed in spiral progression to help your child reach academic milestones, teachers take into consideration how your child learns best and adapt pedagogies in order to deliver the most effective lessons for your child. The curriculum allows for flexibility to support yet challenge your child sufficiently.

Is the curriculum at Mobile Campus in line with MOE’s syllabus?

Our teachers are NIE-trained and are very familiar with the national examinations and curriculum. Especially because lessons are one-to-one, they can closely follow what your child is currently learning in school.

How is Mobile Campus different from other tuition centres?

Mobile Campus harnesses the benefits of technology to ensure that your child learns in the most effective environment possible. We ensure quality online teaching, tailored to your child’s learning. The flexibility of arranging for lessons when they are most needed and receiving feedback through online submission of work and grading is very valuable and unique to our centre.

What qualifications do your teachers have?

Our teachers are all university graduates and have relevant degrees to the subjects that they are teaching. They are NIE-trained and are passionate about their subject content. Our teachers are also trained in classroom management skills and are enthusiastic about working with children!

What are your class schedules like? / How long are your lessons?

Our lessons do not have a fixed schedule! Typically, lessons are once a week, two hours per lesson at your preferred time and day. However, if your child needs more support, there is flexibility to increase the number of lessons per week.

What are the fees like?

Primary Level S$40/hr

Secondary Level S$45/hr

What should I do if I face technical difficulties during the lesson?

Our IT team is on hand to solve any technical difficulties that your child may face during the lesson. In the event that the technical difficulty cannot be resolved, you will not be charged for the lesson and another lesson will be arranged.
